This project explores autonomous driving through deep reinforcement learning by training a  vehicle to complete a 2D driving course. Using a Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) algorithm, the agent learned to navigate efficiently by passing through a sequence of gates, optimizing its policy through points earned based on driving preformance. The model was trained over approximately 16 million time steps, enabling it to develop stable driving behavior. In the future I plan to see how this generalizes across the track layout and compare preformance with other algorithms.
